brief introduction

Estie was born on a Saturday. Interaction Designer from Bogota who, motivated by his profound love of design, moved to London 7 years ago and ever since arriving has had the pleasure to craft digital products for mass consumption.

Estie has been leading design at Fling for the past 2 years where he has had the opportunity to found the company's first design team, injecting design and usability foundations into the core production line. Estie makes crucial product decisions for the use of millions of users every day. He saw Fling grow from 0 to 5 million users.
